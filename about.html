About LabLink

The smartest way to connect students and researchers with labs that match their skills, research interests, and career goals.

What is LabLink?

LabLink is a research lab matching platform built for the modern academic ecosystem. It combines structured data (your skills, education, and field interests) with AI-powered semantic analysis (your research statement) to find the best lab fit for you.

Whether you are an undergraduate looking for your first wet-lab experience, a postbac aiming for a PhD pipeline, or a Master's student exploring industry pathways — LabLink helps you discover labs that align with your unique profile.

How It Works

1. Build your profile

Fill in your education, skills, and research interests from our structured lists.

2. Write your story

Tell us in your own words what excites you about research — this feeds our AI.

3. AI + Algorithm Match

Our system blends rule-based scoring with semantic AI similarity for smarter results.

4. Connect with labs

View your ranked matches and reach out to the labs that fit you best.

Understanding Your Match Score

On your results page, you'll see three badges for each lab. Here is what they mean:

Structure: 78%

Teal Badge – Structure Score

This shows how well your skills, education, and career goals match the lab's requirements. It is based on the structured information you provided — like techniques you know, your field of interest, and your availability.

AI Semantic: 82%

Purple Badge – AI Semantic Score

This is powered by AI. Our system reads your research statement and the lab's description, then measures how similar the topics are. It understands the meaning of your words — not just exact keywords.

✨ Example: If you write "gene editing" and the lab says "CRISPR," the AI recognises they are related and gives you credit.

Match: 79%

Bold Combined Score

This blends both scores (70% structure + 30% AI) to give you an overall match percentage. This is the number you should care about most — it represents the total fit between you and the lab.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AI reading my personal data?

A: Yes, the AI reads your research statement to find matching labs. We do not store or share your personal data with third parties beyond the Cohere AI service used for matching. Your privacy is important — we only process your text temporarily to generate your match results.

What if I don't know many techniques?

A: That's okay! The AI can still find labs based on your research interests. Your research statement matters more than a long list of skills. Focus on describing what you are curious about and what you want to learn.

Why is the AI score sometimes higher than the structure score?

A: The AI may recognise that your research interests align with a lab's work, even if your exact skills don't match perfectly. It is finding hidden connections — for example, a shared focus on neurodegeneration or a common experimental model.

Can I see why a lab was recommended?

A: Yes! Click "Why this match?" on any lab card. You'll see both the structured reasons (like skill matches and field alignment) and the AI explanation (a plain-English summary of why your research statement fits).

How do I get better match results?

A: Write a specific research statement — mention a research topic, techniques you've used (or want to learn), and your long-term goal. The AI works best with detailed, vocabulary-rich text. For example, instead of "I like biology," try "I am interested in how epigenetic modifications regulate stem cell differentiation."
